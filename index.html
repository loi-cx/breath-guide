Common questions about breathwork

Is breathwork safe for everyone? +
Breathwork is generally safe for most people, but certain conditions require modified approaches or medical clearance. We discuss contraindications before starting and always recommend consulting your healthcare provider if you have any medical conditions.
How is this different from meditation apps? +
Unlike passive meditation, breathwork gives your active mind something to focus on. It's perfect for people who struggle with traditional meditation because it works WITH your busy mind rather than asking you to empty it.
What if I've never done breathwork before? +
Perfect! This programme is designed for complete beginners. If you can breathe, you can do breathwork. We start with simple techniques and progress gradually at your own pace.
How much time do I need each day? +
Just 3-5 minutes daily to start. The beauty of breathwork is that it fits into any schedule - you can practice while commuting, before meetings, or as part of your morning routine.
Will this replace my need for therapy or medication? +
Absolutely not. Breathwork is a complementary practice that supports overall wellbeing. It's not a replacement for medical care, therapy, or prescribed medications. We work alongside your existing healthcare team.
